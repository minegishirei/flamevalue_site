Project "Dr.Connect"
Concept (Problems)

A mobile booking medical app that let's you view bookings and schedule appointments.
To create a medical application that had the feel and approach of a tool not just taking all of your important medical information away! (vital)

Roles Played (Impact)

Creating a layout and a mix of colors with Creative Common logos to represent patient, doctor, schedule and appointment.

Tools Used

Java, and mainly inside Android Studio for mobile development along with Adobe Suite for imagery adjustments and color changes. Firebase on the backend for data for sign-up and login access.

  • Objectives

    Creating a medical style application (Medical Card system with NFC)

  • Research

    There was a similar tool on another international market not in Europe but was cluttered with information that was excess.

  • Principles

    Safety and Integrity with GDPR compliance. Extensive research in business report (E.U)

  • Results

    • Easy of Use
    • Minimalistic
    • Approachable
    • Safe
Project "Canine Carnage"
Concept (Problems)

A animation style sequence of a dog and a lone driver on the road. No subplots.
To create a clip that was fun and engaging for the viewer while holding their attention. To bring people into the slapstick world.

Roles Played (Impact)

The visual styling and plot of the story, the reveal of the secondary character and his interaction with the first. The direction of the story and what we can or cant't do.(from a technical standpoint)

Tools Used

Adobe Animate and Suite for the design phase, along with keyframe interactions. Sound effects also added in Audacity.

  • User Flow

    To witness a person in his car along a desert highway come across a dog where he may have ran into but only to avoided and run side by side with the vehicle.

  • Research and Background

    Retro and Vapowave style elements that were used in other forms of clips like "Kung Fury" and music such as David Hasselhoff's True Survivor

  • Drawbacks

    Time allocation of the clip with the project. Using the two minutes of time allocated to the best we have.

  • End Results

    • Bizarre
    • Different
    • Suspense
    • Fun
"Area Agency" Django Development
Concept (Problems)

A music agency booking service that let's users enquire acts (email notification) along with administrator access for artist / agent listing.
To take a music agency style site and give it a more "login" user approach for active promoters which is rarely seen in the industry.

Roles Played (Impact)

Solo project inspired from previous promotional work. Logo design created.

Tools Used

Python3 with a VENV, Model View Controller backend data connected to Postgres.
Coded and compiled in Python3, Bootstrap4 for UI.

  • User Journey

    To have a user log on, search, enquire and book a musical act which then gets logged to the admin area and simultaneously gets confirmation of this to the user.
    Also a dashboard with bookings made.

  • Research and Background

    Dealing with previous agent websites in the past such as Earth Agency which have a minimal and purely white style aesthetic. Observed this and realised I needed to be different.

  • Principles

    To have a more user friendly approach that can be more accomoadating towards newer promoters espeically. A core aim when building the project.

  • End Results

    • Informative
    • Feature-Rich
    • Accessibility
    • Easy
